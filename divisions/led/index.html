Liminal Engineering Division

LED

The Liminal Engineering Division is concerned with liminal phenomena and the dynamics of change within articulated reality. Its work concerns thresholds and their apertures, temporality, continuity, transformation, and the processes through which patterns arise, stabilize, and dissolve.

LED develops, documents, and applies methods for engaging change as a primary expression of reality within articulation.
