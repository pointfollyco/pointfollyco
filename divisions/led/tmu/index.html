Threshold Maintenance Unit

LED / TMU

The Threshold Maintenance Unit is concerned with thresholds and their apertures. Its work focuses on aperture formation, stabilization, boundary conditions, and the effects of pattern introduction, stabilization, and resolution on aperture dynamics.

TMU develops, documents, and applies methods for identifying, cultivating, and navigating threshold conditions and aperture phenomena.

Public materials are being prepared for release.
