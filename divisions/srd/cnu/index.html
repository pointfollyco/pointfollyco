Ceremonial Nonsense Unit

SRD / CNU

The Ceremonial Nonsense Unit is concerned with ceremonial systems, participatory architectures, and the intentional arrangement of symbolic structures. Its work concerns correspondences, actions, environments, sequences, and other elements through which experiential patterns may arise, stabilize, and resolve.

CNU develops, documents, and applies methods for designing, conducting, and evaluating symbolic arrangements, with particular attention to how correspondences, actions, environments, and sequences influence articulation.

Public materials are being prepared for release.
