Memetic Ecology Unit

SRD / MEU

The Memetic Ecology Unit is concerned with memetic populations, habitats, and ecologies. Its work concerns the emergence, propagation, domestication, mutation, and interaction of memetic constructs across human, machine, and environmental contexts.

MEU develops methods for observing, documenting, cultivating, and managing memetic populations and environments.
